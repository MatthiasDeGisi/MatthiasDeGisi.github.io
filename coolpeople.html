Cool People

Surprisingly, I'm not the only cool person on the internet. This page links out to a few other cool people who I'd highly recommend checking out.

I don't know how much Deiniol is OK with me saying about him, but I know for a fact that he's cool with me sharing one of his projects, Tiny Gardens. Tiny Gardens is a god-game that Deiniol coded himself in Haxe, and adapted it to be playable on the web. A steam release is planned for some point in the future, but for now, check it out at deiniol.ca.

Brandon Brown is the sole reason that this website exists. He taught me everything that I know about web development and design, and put up with my numerous questions all throughout the semester. If you ever find yourself in a class taught by him, count yourself lucky. He has 2 websites to check out: brandonb.ca and ve7tzb.ca.
